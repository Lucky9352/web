Refunds & Cancellations Policy

Effective Date: July 31, 2025

1. General Policy

All ticket sales are final and non-refundable for user-initiated reasons (e.g., change of plans, personal emergencies).

2. Event-Initiated Changes

For event cancellations, postponements, or significant modifications, refunds are subject to the specific organizer's policy.

Note: In case of event changes, the event organizer will communicate directly with ticket holders via the registered email address.

3. Denied Entry

No refunds are provided for denied entry due to:

  • Late arrival
  • Failure to produce a valid ticket/ID
  • Violation of event rules

4. Payment Issues

For duplicate or failed payments where a charge occurred without a ticket, users must contact support within 24 hours. After verification, a ticket will be reissued or a refund will be processed within 5-10 working days.

5. Ticket Transferability

Tickets are generally non-transferable and non-exchangeable unless specified by the organizer.

6. Policy Updates

This policy is subject to change, with the latest version available on the website.

If you have any questions about our refund and cancellation policies, please contact our support team at info@tapnex.tech.
