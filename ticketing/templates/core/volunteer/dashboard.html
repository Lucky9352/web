Volunteer Instructions

1. Scanning Tickets: Click "Open Scanner" to access the QR code scanner. Allow camera access when prompted.

2. Validation Process: Point the camera at the attendee's ticket QR code. The system will automatically scan and validate.

3. Success/Failure: Watch for the green success or red failure message, and listen for the audio confirmation.

4. Multiple Entries: For tickets allowing multiple people (e.g., couple/group tickets), the system will show how many people are allowed entry.

5. Issues: If there are technical problems, contact the event organizer immediately.
